```

- Line weight: 3pt thick black lines (beginner-friendly thickness)

- Style: Adult coloring book page, professional quality
```

### Prompt 1: Single Rose Blooms (Designs 1-15)

```
Create a simple adult coloring book page featuring a single large rose bloom in mandala style.

CRITICAL REQUIREMENTS:
- THICK black outlines (3pt weight) - beginner friendly
- LARGE open spaces for easy coloring
- Simple 6-point radial symmetry
- Maximum 2-3 petal layers only
- Clear, distinct shapes with no fine details
- Center should be a simple spiral or dot pattern
- 5-6 oversized petals, rounded edges
- No background elements or textures
- Pure white background
- Black and white line art only
- Square format 1:1 aspect ratio

AVOID: thin lines, intricate details, small spaces, complex patterns, shading, gradients

Style: Clean minimalist mandala, easy adult coloring book, stress-free design
--ar 1:1 --v 6
```
