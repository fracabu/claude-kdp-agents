3. **Paper Recommendation:** White paper strongly recommended for HARD designs to maximize contrast for fine lines.

4. **Marketing Angle:** Position as "The Ultimate Challenge" - the capstone of the series for dedicated colorists.

5. **Pricing Justification:** $9.99/Euro 9.99 reflects premium complexity and extended engagement time.
