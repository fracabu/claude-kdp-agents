## KEY DIFFERENTIATION STRATEGY

### Unique Selling Points

1. **104 Pages** (vs competitors' 30-40 pages) = **Superior value proposition**
2. **Hybrid Positioning:** "Architecture in Scenic Settings" = targets TWO proven markets
3. **Cultural Diversity:** Italian, French, Spanish, German architecture = broad appeal
4. **"Cozy" Aesthetic:** Taps into 2025 trend (Jade Summer's "Cozy Europe" success)
5. **Advanced Detail Level:** "Detailed designs" = targets less saturated advanced colorist segment
6. **Italian Focus (IT Market):** Venice, Florence, Tuscany, Amalfi = cultural pride

### Content Distribution (Recommended)

- 20% Italian architecture (Venice, Florence, Rome, Tuscany, Amalfi)
- 20% French architecture (Paris, Provence, Loire Valley)
- 15% Spanish/Portuguese (Barcelona, Andalusia, Porto)
- 15% British/Irish (London, Cotswolds, Edinburgh, Dublin)
- 15% Central European (Prague, Vienna, Amsterdam, Bruges)
- 15% Coastal/Scenic (Mediterranean villages, harbor towns, countryside)

### Style Guidelines

- **Balance:** 60% buildings + 40% scenic elements (trees, flowers, sky, cobblestones)
- **Detail Level:** Medium-detailed (not overwhelming, not too simple)
- **Avoid:** Modern skyscrapers, cars, people (faces), text/signs
- **Emphasize:** Windows, doors, architectural details, charming atmosphere
